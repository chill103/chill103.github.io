Captivating Majestic Hummingbird, LLC.

Captivating Majestic Hummingbird is a professinal birdwatching company for hummingbirds. We strive to ensure that new species are found and documented while also tracking their migritory and reproductive patterns to best determine the ways that society can help ensure the continuance of the hummingbird. Here's some facts about us!

  • Started in 1903 in Sparta, North Carolina
  • Professionally Certified Birdwatching Company
  • Specilize in different Hummingbird species
  • We have been featured in Birdwatcher's Yearly 16 times
  • Trained to care for and rehabilitate injured Hummingbirds
